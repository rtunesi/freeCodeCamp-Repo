Introducing the NexaControl Pro X

Engineered for precision. Built for champions.

The NexaControl Pro X is our most advanced wireless gaming controller yet. With ultra-low latency, adaptive triggers, and a battery life that outlasts your longest sessions, it's the last controller you'll ever need.

Precision Control

Our redesigned thumbsticks use Hall Effect sensors to eliminate stick drift permanently. Combined with a 1000Hz polling rate, every movement registers exactly as intended — giving you the edge in competitive play.

Adaptive Triggers

Experience dynamic resistance feedback that responds to in-game events. Feel the tension of drawing a bowstring, the kickback of a weapon, or the grip of a racing car — all through the triggers alone.

All-Day Battery

A 3000mAh battery delivers up to 40 hours of uninterrupted play on a single charge. USB-C fast charging gives you 3 hours of playtime from just 15 minutes plugged in.
